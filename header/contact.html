Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and processes.

What types of projects do you typically work on? +

We specialize in AI solutions, custom software development, mobile apps, and web applications across various industries. Our team has experience with startups, mid-sized businesses, and enterprise clients. We particularly excel in projects that require innovative technical solutions and complex problem-solving.

How do you determine project costs? +

Project costs are determined based on scope, complexity, and timeline. We offer both fixed-price and time-and-materials engagement models. After an initial consultation where we understand your requirements, we'll provide a detailed proposal with transparent pricing. For larger projects, we often recommend starting with a discovery phase to accurately estimate costs.

What is your typical project timeline? +

Timelines vary depending on project scope. A minimum viable product (MVP) might take 2-3 months, while more complex solutions could take 6-12 months. We follow agile methodologies, delivering working software in regular iterations. This allows for flexibility and ensures we're always moving toward your business goals.

Do you offer ongoing support and maintenance? +

Yes, we offer comprehensive support and maintenance packages tailored to your needs. Our services include bug fixes, performance optimization, security updates, feature enhancements, and 24/7 monitoring for critical systems. Many clients find our retainer model provides the best value for ongoing development needs.

How do you ensure the security of our data? +

Security is our top priority. We implement industry-standard encryption, secure coding practices, regular security audits, and compliance with relevant regulations (GDPR, HIPAA, etc. as applicable). All team members undergo security training, and we're happy to sign NDAs or specific security agreements to protect your sensitive information.
